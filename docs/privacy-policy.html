Privacy Policy

App: FaceShot-BuildingBlock

Package: com.n0tez.app

Effective date: 2026-05-03

This Privacy Policy explains how FaceShot-BuildingBlock handles user data for the Android application distributed through Google Play.

Developer and Contact

Privacy questions or data requests can be submitted through the public repository issue tracker: github.com/applesaucetoaboss1413/FaceShot-BuildingBlock-Formerly-known-as-n0tez-/issues

What the App Accesses on Your Device

The app may request access to the following device capabilities when you use matching features:

  • Photos, videos, and audio files that you choose to open, edit, save, export, or share.
  • Microphone access for voice recording and audio editing.
  • Camera access for camera-based media workflows.
  • Overlay permission for the floating note widget.
  • Accessibility access for the optional text capture feature that reads visible text behind the floating note.
  • Notification permission for app notifications on supported Android versions.

Data Stored on the Device

Notes, media imports, exports, recordings, edited files, overlay preferences, and PIN-related settings are primarily stored on the device. Sensitive settings such as PIN-related values use Android encrypted storage where applicable.

Text captured through the accessibility-based text capture feature is processed for the feature you invoke and inserted into your note on the device.

Data Collected Off the Device

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and stability monitoring. When a crash or serious runtime error occurs, Crashlytics may collect:

  • Crash logs and diagnostic information.
  • App version and build information.
  • Device and OS metadata relevant to the crash.
  • A Crashlytics installation identifier used to group and measure crashes.

The app does not use advertising SDKs, does not sell personal data, and does not collect contacts, location, payment data, health data, or account credentials.

How Data Is Used

  • To provide app features such as note taking, media editing, recording, sharing, and export.
  • To keep your content available on your device and in your chosen storage locations.
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve app stability.
  • To meet platform, security, and legal requirements.

Sharing and Third Parties

User data is not sold. Crash and diagnostic data is processed by Google Firebase Crashlytics acting as a service provider for app stability reporting. User-initiated sharing, such as when you export or share a file yourself, happens only because you choose that action.

Security

  • Runtime permissions are requested before access to protected device data where Android supports it.
  • PIN-related settings use encrypted storage on device where implemented.
  • Crash diagnostics transmitted by Firebase are encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

Retention and Deletion

Content that the app stores locally remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Exported media remains where you save it unless you delete it.

Crash diagnostics are retained according to Firebase Crashlytics retention and processing rules. You can submit a deletion request through the contact channel above, and requests will be handled to the extent the data can be identified and deleted within the Firebase tooling used for this app.

Children

The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children through its own backend services.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the app's data practices change. The latest version will remain available at this public URL.
